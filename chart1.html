Which streaming service has the highest number of movies?

The charts below represent content volume (number of movies released) per streaming service and this data set is provided by Kaggle. Video Streaming Services such as Netflix, Prime Video, Hulu and DisneyPlus are fundamentally changing the viewing habits of consumers of all ages.

The charts clearly depict that Amazon's Video on Demand Service ranks first among companies with the biggest impact on digital video. To learn more about the content libraries of the four streaming giants is the soul objective of this project. This will help potential subscribers in choosing the best streaming service based on content quality (IMDb score), Genre, Rating, Country and Language and yearly releases.
Content Volume Chart Description
Left Bar Chart shows content volume for each streaming provider by Number of movies won
Right Pie Chart shows four streaming services by Number of movies in percentages

You may click on the bar to interactively view corresponding data about that streaming service.
